How to Make Your Own Football Team?

Simply drag and drop players to create formations(drag and drop feature is not available on mobile). Or choose a football formation template from the configuration area which has all the popular offensive or defensive tactics like 4-2-3-1, 4-4-2, 3-5-2 or diamond formation. To enter a player name click Click to edit or choose Players tab and edit the Name column.

You will see a configuration area with three options: Style, Players, and My Lineups.

In Style section you can quickly customize and create your own soccer team jersey, formation and field template. Currently there are four different kit(including shirt or circle shaped) designs and five football pitch templates.To build a 5-a-side, a 6-a-side, a 7-a-side, a 8-a-side or a 9-a-side formation change "Player Count" value. Any number between (and including) 1-11 can be chosen. Most of the time users go with eleven-man football lineups. Wikipedia has a great page about formations. You could also check out articles section of the site to learn more about defensive and offensive formations.

You have an option to show player positions. As you drag and drop a player the position label will automatically change. You can also turn this off if you want plain jerseys without text. How to Get the Soccer Lineup Image?

If you use Chrome or Firefox, the app also works as a graphic creator. By clicking "Download Image" button you can download the lineup as a PNG image and use it anywhere. For the best quality screenshots use your own device to get a native screenshot. On Windows you can use Snipping Tool that comes with the operating system. On a Mac device press Command + Shift + 4 and select the formation area you would like to save.

Players section lists all the players on the field. It has three tabs: General, Icons and Subs. Under the General tab name, jersey number and arrow can be edited for each player. To add arrows click Players tab on the left side and choose the arrow you would like to attach to a player. This is a good option for creating advanced soccer formations and to show the area a player influences. It lets you use the builder as a tactics board and gives more idea about the player's role.
